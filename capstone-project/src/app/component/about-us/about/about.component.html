About us

We started our business as traders in the 1960s, then moved to distribution; after that, we started a business as a manufacturer of high-quality cosmetics products in 2004. At A.S Cosmetics, we believe that beauty comes from within all of us. Our luxurious cosmetics line captures great-looking skin with gorgeous colors and quality ingredients. Our products are designed to let your inner beauty shine through. Our main products include hair color cream, shampoo, hair conditioner, and depilatory products. We also have automatic filling production lines. All products are made under the safety ordinance of China, the USA, and Europe. We have built good relationships with domestic and overseas clients through our high-quality products, first-class management system, and excellent after-sales service.
